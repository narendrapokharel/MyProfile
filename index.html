About Me

Narendra Pokharel, hailing from Nepal, is a dedicated Agricultural Economics student at the University of Hohenheim in Germany. With a fervent interest in food and agricultural policy, international trade, rural development, and food security, he passionately explores the intricate connections within global food systems. Drawing on his diverse Nepalese background, Narendra envisions using his expertise to contribute to the fields of food security, rural development, and international trade, positioning himself as a promising voice in shaping the future of agricultural economics.
